Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Unitalsh?

Unitalsh is an online student guidance platform designed to help students make informed decisions about their future by providing valuable information on universities, careers, and test preparation.

2. How do I use the Merit Calculator?

Simply enter your academic details, including marks and subject information, and the calculator will determine your merit for different universities based on their requirements.

3. Can I get career counseling on Unitalsh?

Yes! We offer free and paid career counseling services to help you navigate your career choices and understand which fields align with your strengths and interests.

4. How do I apply for universities through Unitalsh?

You can browse universities, view admission requirements, and apply directly through the platform. We also assist you with filling out the admission forms and submitting them to the respective universities.

5. How does the AI Career Suggestion work?

After completing a short questionnaire, the AI system analyzes your academic background and personal preferences to suggest suitable career paths based on high-demand fields in the job market.

6. Is there a fee for using Unitalsh?

Our platform is free to use for most features. However, career counseling and some specialized services may have a fee.
